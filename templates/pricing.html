Common questions

How does the P&L tracker work?

Enter what you paid for each skin on your dashboard. CS2 Tracker compares it to the current market price and shows your profit or loss per skin and in total.

How do price alerts work?

Set a target price for any skin. When the Steam market price drops below it, you'll get an email immediately. Pro gives you unlimited alerts.
